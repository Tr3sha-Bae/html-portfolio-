The Best Movies According to Patricia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Roses and Ivy

A very interesting series focused on the traditional family life. A man who had to singlehandedly raise his two daughters and how they navigate through life issues.

Hire A Woman

It talked about how a guy who was not social had to hire a lady on a get together in order to get back to his ex whom unknown to him, he hadn't gotten over.

Baby Farm

It talked about an institution disguised to be helping young girls who got pregnant out of wedlock but unknown to the public was selliing the babies of these young girls and making them a baby factory.
